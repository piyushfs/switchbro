How to Set Up TOTP Secret

Follow these steps to configure TOTP for your brokers in our extension.

This guide will help you extract the TOTP secret from your broker's website and set it up in an authentication app as well as our extension.

For Fyers

  1. Step 1: Follow these steps to create a new TOTP (If you have already enabled the TOTP, disable and create a new one)
  2. Step 2: Copy the key secret displayed on the page, and paste it into the extension
  3. Step 3: Scan the QR code in your authentication app on mobile.
  4. Step 4: Provide the code and the Pin and enable the TOTP

For Zerodha

  1. Step 1: Follow these steps to create a new TOTP (If you have already enabled the TOTP, disable and create a new one)
  2. Step 2: Copy the key secret from below the QR on the page, and paste it into the extension
  3. Step 3: Scan the QR code in your authentication app on mobile.
  4. Step 4: Provide the code and the Password and enable the TOTP
